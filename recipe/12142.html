Sweet Potato Pie I

Original Ingredients
  • 1 (1 pound) sweet potato
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust

Trending Words

sweeter

They come out sweeter, and I prefer the consistency.

runny

The mixture will be very runny before you add it to the pie crust.

Common Modifications

decrease milk

I use half and half instead of milk (if on hand) as well as a 1/4 tsp clove.

(helps with texture) I used 1/2 cup of heavy whipping cream (helps with texture too) instead of milk.

decrease sugar

I cut back the sugar to 3/4 cup since the sugar didnt leach out by boiling.

I used Splenda instead of sugar (3/4 C) and added a spash of lemon juice and extra vanilla.

decrease white sugar

Cut the sugar to 2/3 cup and use half white sugar, half brown.

I also did brown sugar instead of white sugar.

reviews are mixed for nutmeg

Substitutions: use 1/2 the nutmeg; replace w/ cinnamon and use 1/2 white & 1/2 light brown sugar.

I would add more sugar and add more nutmeg and cinamon to add to the flavor.

Also make sure those half teaspoons of nutmeg and cinnamon are heaping half teaspoons.

I used 1 tsp of pumpkin pie spice (recipe on AR) instead of the nutmeg & cinnamon.

increase cinnamon

I add cinnamon, a little allspice, a little ginger, and a little ground clove.

I did add a little more cinnamon and nutmeg and a pinch of ginger.

decrease regular milk

I use evaporated milk instead of regular milk.

Common Additions/Suggestions

flour

I also add a 1 tbsp flour to the mixture to thicken up a bit.

It was a little runny, so instead of adding flour we just added more sweet potato!

spices

Added a little more spices, but overall very good.

i also doubled the spices, and added 1/2 teaspoon each of ground ginger and ground cloves.

lemon juice

i took others' advice to add 1 tbsp lemon juice and some flour to thicken it up.

ginger

I also used 2 teaspoons of vanilla, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, and added 1/4 teaspoon ginger to it.

I don't like nutmeg so I substituted ginger and used sweetened condensed milk and it came out AMAZEBALLS!!

yams

Do NOT use store bought yams it doesnt set right it is not good.

Some people may be using sweet potatoes(yellowish flesh) instead of yams (orange flesh).
