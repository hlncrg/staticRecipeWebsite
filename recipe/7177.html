Bread Pudding II

Original Ingredients
  • 6 slices day-old bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Common Modifications

decrease milk

(Fresher bread uses less milk... staler bread needs more).

I used half&half instead of the milk, and used 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1/4 cup white sugar.

decrease sugar

First, I used a heaping half cup of sugar, not 3/4 cup to cut it down just a smidgen.

Also replaced the sugar with a reduced amount of honey.

decrease egg

It needed one less egg.

Common Additions/Suggestions

eggs

Following recommendations of previous users, I added 3 eggs only; baked for 50 min.

Also, I doubled the recipe BUT only used five eggs and everyone thought it was perfect.

I used all Splenda, as I'm diabetic, and cut back the eggs to 3, based on other reviews.

I used 2 eggs and 2 egg whites instead of the called for 3 eggs.

nutmeg

I also used brown sugar intead of white sugar and added nutmeg to the whole.

I added a half teaspoon of nutmeg.

brown sugar

I also added 1/4c of brown sugar.

Specifically, I used 1/2 C white sugar and then a bit more than 1/4 C brown sugar.

I also took other reviewer's suggestions and did half brown sugar and half white sugar.

Substitute 1/4 cup of brown sugar for regular sugar.

cranberries

Or add Cranberries, blueberries and raspberries.

pecans

Added roasted pecans in with the raisins and used splenda-brown and white.
