One Bowl Chocolate Cake III

Original Ingredients
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Common Modifications

decrease sugar

I only reduced the sugar by half, the rest, i followed the recipe exactly.

I also used substitute sugar and only used water (no milk).

decrease oil

I have also substituted half the oil with applesauce and I couldn't tell the difference.

Instead of oil I used applesauce and instead eggs I used egg substitute.

decrease milk

I opted for replacing half the milk with yogurt, it adds moisture.

I've also substituted the milk with soy milk on a few occasions and it turns out just fine.
