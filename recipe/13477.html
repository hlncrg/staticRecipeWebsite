Double Layer Pumpkin Cheesecake

Original Ingredients
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed

I made it a little lighter using a reduced fat graham cracker crust and reduced fat cream cheese.

Common Additions/Suggestions

pumpkin pie filling

To make it even easier, I just use pumpkin pie filling instead of canned pumpkin and the individual spices.

pumpkin pie spice

Also, instead of adding cloves and nutmeg I added 1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice.

I added 1.5 teaspoons of vanilla, 2 cups of sugar, and used extra pumpkin pie spice.

I substituted Pumpkin Pie spice instead of the individual spices.

sour cream

I increased all ingredients by 1/3 and added a/3 cup sour cream.

eggs

I completely forgot to add the eggs, but it still came out marvelously!
