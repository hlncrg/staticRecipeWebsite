Peanut Butter Bars I

Original Ingredients
  • 1 cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 4 tablespoons peanut butter

Common Modifications

decrease butter

For the peanut butter part, we only used a half of cup of butter instead of a whole cup.

CHANGES I MADE: Instead of 1 cup of butter, I used a little more than 1/2 a cup.

reviews are mixed for graham crackers

I decreased the butter to 1 1/2 sticks, and used 14 graham crackers, and milk chocolate chips.

Then I added both to the graham crackers and powdered sugar.

I will follow Lisa H's suggestion and add about 1/4 cup more of graham crackers for a firmer crust.

I have also substituted the graham crackers with shortbread cookies.

Common Additions/Suggestions

milk chocolate chips

I also used half semi-sweet chips and half milk chocolate chips for the chocolate layer.

Only modification I made was to sub milk chocolate chips for the semi-sweet variety.

sticks

Awesome with jiffy to One and a half sticks of butter was PLENTY!

Oh, and I used 1.5 sticks (3/4 cup) butter instead of 2 sticks (1 cup).

natural peanut butter

I had read previous posts where people had used natural peanut butter and it had not set up.

milk chocolate

I used half milk chocolate and half semi-sweet chocolate chips.

I do not care for semi-sweet anything so I always sub with milk chocolate.

stick

I made everything as listed except I used 3/4 cup butter instead of 1 stick.
