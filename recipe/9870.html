Easy Sugar Cookies

Original Ingredients
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Common Additions/Suggestions

almond extract

To the original ingredients I added 1/4 tsp almond extract and a dash of cinnamon.

margarine

I used half butter, half margarine and made sure both were sufficiently softened.

Use butter instead of margarine.

cinnamon

Before baking I sprinkled a mix of cinnamon and sugar over the cookie and flattened it a bit.

salt

Cookies have much better flavor if you add 1/4 tsp salt to dough.

I added half a teaspoon of salt because I don't like cookies without it.

We tried adding milk to the recipe to add moisture and that seems to work better.

However, I would add some extra milk and vanilla and put a little less sugar.
