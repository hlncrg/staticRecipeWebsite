Best Big, Fat, Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookie

Original Ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Common Modifications

decrease chocolate chips

Very good cookie, but WAY too many chocolate chips and I like chocolate.

Maybe using M&Ms instead of chocolate chips would intice them.

decrease butter

I also used half butter, half shortening to make them cool softer.

I'll stick with my old recipe (which is almost identical, except using Crisco shortening instead of butter.)

increase flour

I doubled the batch (I needed a lot of cookies) and added 1/4 C. more flour.

If you dip you use more flour.

decrease sugar

Also reduced the sugar to 3/4 cup brown, and 1/4 cup white.

Changes made: I subbed in more brown sugar instead of white and I only added half the chocolate chips.

decrease chips

Way too many chips for the recipe.

The only thing I changed was instead of 2 cups off chips I used 1 1/2.

reviews are mixed for vanilla

I used two teaspoon vanilla and used half semi-sweet and half dark chocolate chips.

I added 2 teaspoons of vanilla, chocolate chips, and oats, and they were delicous!

We also doubled the vanilla and baking powder.

I may make these again with less vanilla.

I used white choc chips and instead of vanilla, I used 1 tsp of almond extract.

Common Additions/Suggestions

walnuts

I added a cup of chopped walnuts because I like nutty cookies.

I even took out 1/4 of the dough stirred in some chopped walnuts.

I substituted 1 cup chopped walnuts for 1 cup of chocolate chips.

salt

I used salted butter so I did not add the extra 1/2 teaspoon of salt.

However, I did make two minor adjustments- only 1/4 cup white sugar and a smidge more salt.

If you use salted butter, omit the salt.

I did as others suggested and added salted butter instead of salt and unsalted butter.

margarine

Subbed oil for the butter due to roommate's sensitivity and added 1 T of margarine.

My only substitution in this recipe was that I used half butter and half margarine.

I substituted margarine for butter and I added chopped walnuts!

white sugar

Then I added the 1/2 cup of white sugar as the recipe asks for.

Next time, I will try to reduce the 1/2 cup white sugar to 1/4 cup.

I now substitute the white sugar with splenda and the flour with whole wheat flour.

milk chocolate chips

I use half milk chocolate chips and semi-sweet chocolate chips.

pecans

I would also add toasted pecans since I love them in a chocolate chip cookie.

I doubled the recipe and added pecans.

I didn't have any chocolate chips but substituted pecans instead.
