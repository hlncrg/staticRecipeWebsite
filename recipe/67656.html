Cake Balls

Original Ingredients
  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix
  • 1 (16 ounce) container prepared chocolate frosting
  • 1 (3 ounce) bar chocolate flavored confectioners coating

Common Additions/Suggestions

strawberry

I used strawberry cake mix with white frosing then dipped in dark chocolate.

german chocolate

I dipped half in German chocolate and the other half in white chocolate then topped with coconut.

chocolate chips

Do NOT use chocolate chips for the covering - way too thick!

sprinkles

I added sprinkles to the top of mine to make them more festive.

Instead of sprinkles, I dusted with powdered sugar and threw around some sliced strawberrys for a beautiful presentation.

shortening

For chocolate chips, add in 1/2 T shortening for each cup of chips.
