Common Additions/Suggestions

chocolate chips

It is so rich that I just sprinkled chocolate chips over it as it came out of the oven.

I used about a half cup chocolate chips sprinkled on top before baking instead of frosting.

I did substitute chocolate chips for the walnuts.

egg

Do not add an egg if you want a moist, fudgy brownie.

eggs

Adding the 2 eggs makes this more like a very dense cake than brownies.

They were moist, chocolatey and just more than I anticipated for a recipe without eggs.

whole wheat flour

I also used only 1 cup of all purpose flour and added 1 cup of whole wheat flour.

butter

I even substitute half the butter with yogurt, and do all wheat flour, and they still turn out decadent!

I used Earth Balance butter substitute instead of butter for the frosting and made it totally vegan!

salt

I also did like other reviewers and added the salt to the zucchini instead of the dry mix.

Next time, I will modify the recipe to add more liquid (egg, applesauce) and use less salt.
