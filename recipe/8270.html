Sam's Famous Carrot Cake

Original Ingredients
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 cups shredded carrots
  • 1 cup flaked coconut
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice
  • 1 cup raisins

Common Modifications

decrease oil

I think I should have drained the pineapple and reduced the oil.

I replace the oil with applesauce except for 1/8 cup and it makes it lower in fat.

decrease walnuts

For a smoother texture, I would omit the walnuts.

Used pecans instead of walnuts and frosted it with cream cheese frosting and topped with toasted pecans.

decrease buttermilk

Instead of buttermilk, I used 1 tbsp lemon juice to sour regular milk.

reviews are mixed for pineapple

Mom use to make this & I remembered it had crushed pineapple and coconut!

Next time I will add the pineapple but not the juice, the cake was just a little too moist.

I accidently doubled the pineapple and it made it taste like bread pudding!

Only had half the pineapple needed, so I used applesauce to make up the difference.

I used one shredded apple instead of the pineapple, since I didn't have it.

decrease vegetable oil

For a lighter texture I might use less vegetable oil next time.

I also used 1/4 cup each of olive oil and applesauce instead of the 3/4 cup of vegetable oil.

Common Additions/Suggestions

nutmeg

I also decreased the cinnamon to 1 1/2 tsp and added the nutmeg and allspice (1/4 tsp each).

cream cheese frosting ii

Didn't change a thing in the recipe, but added Cream Cheese Frosting II.

Doubled the Cream Cheese Frosting II to ice 3 layers and it was perfect!

cream cheese frosting

But after adding cream cheese frosting it was perfect.

I make 2 rounds and slice them lengthwise for 4 layers and more cream cheese frosting.

I think if I make it again I will add more spices and not use any cream cheese frosting.

pecans

I used apple sauce in place of the oil, and added some chopped toasted pecans, and it was delicious.

I omitted the raisins as my family doesn't care for them and substituted chopped pecans for walnuts.

milk

I also added 3/4 C of powdered milk with the dry ingredients.

I didn't have buttermilk so I substituted with milk 3/4 cup and 1 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar.

whole wheat flour

Next time I might try using half whole wheat flour and replace more of the oil with applesauce.
