Big Soft Ginger Cookies

Original Ingredients
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup margarine, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Common Modifications

decrease water

I don't usually like to change the instructions, but I did omit the water and they turned out perfect!

*edit* On the second batch I took the advice of another reviewer and used orange juice instead of water.

decrease margarine

The recipe I have has more flour, more baking soda, less margarine, and a little more molasses.

I used all butter instead of margarine.

increase flour

I saw in a few reviews that they added more flour, or they omitted the water (liquid) altogether..

increase ginger

I ADDED 5 teaspoon of EXTRA ginger and 3/4 teaspoon fresh black pepper to make them spicy enough.

decrease white sugar

I also replaced half of the white sugar with dark brown sugar and added a teaspoon of vanilla.

I also used 1/2 c white sugar and 1/2 c light brown sugar instead of 1 c white sugar.

increase spices

Loved the texture....followed the instructions exactly but 'heaped' the measures when adding the spices.

I also doubled up on the spices, used butter instead of margarine and melted the butter.

Common Additions/Suggestions

butter

I combine all the dry ingredients in a seperate bowl first then add them to the butter mixture.

I doubled the recipe, and used half butter, half butter-flavored shortening (in place of the margarine.)

Didn't spread much (due to less butter), but rose up nice and fluffy.

I sub butter for margarine and always throw in a little extra spice.

nutmeg

I also used orange juice instead of water and added about 1/2 teaspoon of nutmeg.

I definitely recommend adding more nutmeg.

I added half a teaspoon of nutmeg and did not add orange juice.

We had no cloves, so we substituted it for nutmeg.

spice

I will add more spice and molasses next time.

I might even add a little extra spice next time to really spice it up.

vanilla

I also added about a teaspoon of vanilla.

fresh ginger

I also added 2 tsp fresh ginger because I love the flavour.

doubled the recipe and used fresh ginger.

I substituted fresh ginger for the ground ginger and used coconut oil instead of margarine.

honey

I used butter instead of margarine and since we can't get molasses here, I substituted honey.
